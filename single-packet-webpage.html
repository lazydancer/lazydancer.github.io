Computers communicate by sending packets of information. Each webpage is a combination of multiple packets which are re-combined. As an experiment, I wanted to fit an article into one packet of information. The size of one of these packets is around 1400 bytes of data. This was determined from ethernet when balancing throughput and reliability.

While working on this I found two cool tools: primitive.js for the creation of the svg and SVGOMG a tool to reduce the size of svg images
